About Gabrielle

Gabrielle Calise is a Gainesville-based storyteller studying journalism at the University of Florida. She worked as an editorial intern at Tower Publications, Tampa Bay Newspapers and The Gainesville Sun. She is currently completing multimedia journalism projects as an intern at the University of Florida News Department.

Gabrielle has contributed articles and photographs to Tower Publications (Our Town Magazine and Senior Times Magazine), Tampa Bay Newspapers' eight regional publications, The Independent Florida Alligator, the Tampa Bay Times, the Gainesville Sun, the Fine Print and Sparks Magazine. She also worked as a producer and anchor for WUFT's News In 90. Gabrielle is a Journalism and Communications Ambassador at the University of Florida and the vice president of the Florida Magazine Student Association. An avid music fan, Gabrielle also founded the music blog Swamp Sounds to profile the entertainment scene in Gainesville.
